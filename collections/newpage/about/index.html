Who We Are

Clothing with a quiet point of view
made for repeat wearing

Designed around fabric, fit, and restraint

KIWAD builds occasionwear and everyday sets for wardrobes that need polish without excess. Each drop starts with textile, proportion, and colour before anything is cut.

Our silhouettes are shaped for movement: clean kurtas, considered co-ords, detailed dupattas, and jewellery-led styling moments that can move between family gatherings, workdays, and late dinners.

We keep the edit focused so every piece earns its place. The result is clothing that feels elevated on arrival and still useful seasons later.

— KIWAD Studio
